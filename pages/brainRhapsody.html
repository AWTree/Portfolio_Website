About

This is a top-down shooter game made by Unity. Players will be able to switch between the roles of dark wizard, cowboy, scuba diver, astronaut, and mob boss. Each character has a separate weapon and attack. Each time the player switches to a different character, the background and enemies will change. For example, when the player switches to the mob boss, the scene will turn black and white and the skeletons throwing flying daggers will be refreshed in the scene as the enemy. And when the player switches to the astronaut, the scene will turn blue and with alien enemies refreshing.
