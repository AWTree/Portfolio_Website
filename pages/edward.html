About

This is a Game Bible of a video game concept I conceived. The story is about the protagonist's family being bloodied due to a conflict with another noble family. The severely injured protagonist is rescued by an old hunter, and under the effect of a mysterious potion, the protagonist recovers from his injuries and gains a stronger body and strength. But the hunter, for his own selfish purposes, directs the protagonist's anger towards a vampire count and makes him believe that he is the enemy of his family. In the process of revenge, the protagonist commits many murders. The story explores the devastating consequences of hatred.
